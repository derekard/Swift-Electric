Our Story

Power you can count on.

Swift Electric is a modern electrical services company serving homeowners and businesses across Durham Region. We're built on forward-thinking solutions and clear communication, with work planned to hold up over time.

Too many people have a bad electrician story: the no-show, the surprise invoice, the mess left behind. We built Swift to be the opposite. Clear estimates before we start. Clean job sites. Every circuit tested before we call it done.

From a single outlet to a full service upgrade or EV charger, the standard never changes, safe, code-compliant work, explained in plain language.
