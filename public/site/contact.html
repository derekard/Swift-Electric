FAQ

Questions, answered.

Do you charge for quotes?

No, quotes are free. We'll give you a clear, up-front estimate before any work begins so there are no surprises on the invoice.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Swift Electric is a licensed electrical contractor (ECRA/ESA) and carries full liability and WSIB coverage. We pull permits and handle ESA inspections where required.

How fast can you come out?

For standard work we usually book within a few days. Need it sooner? Let us know when you reach out and we'll do our best to fit you in.

What areas do you serve?

The entire east end of the GTA, from Scarborough through Durham Region to Clarington: Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, Oshawa, Bowmanville, Markham, Stouffville, Uxbridge, Port Perry and more. Not sure? Just call.

Do you install EV chargers?

Absolutely, it's one of our specialties. We size the charger to your vehicle and panel, run a clean dedicated circuit, and walk you through any available rebates.
