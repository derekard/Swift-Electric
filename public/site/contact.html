FAQ

Questions, answered.

How do quote requests work?

Share the job details online. We'll review the scope and provide a clear, up-front estimate before any work begins so there are no surprises on the invoice.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Swift Electric is a licensed electrical contractor (ECRA/ESA) and carries full liability and WSIB coverage. We pull permits and handle ESA inspections where required.

How should I share timing?

Include your preferred dates, access notes, and any urgency in the request. We'll use that context when following up.

What areas do you serve?

The entire east end of the GTA, from Scarborough through Durham Region to Clarington: Pickering, Ajax, Whitby, Oshawa, Bowmanville, Markham, Stouffville, Uxbridge, Port Perry and more. Not sure? Send a request with your area.

Do you install EV chargers?

Yes. Include your charger model, panel details if you have them, and parking location so the team can plan the next step.
