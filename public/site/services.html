Our Services

Licensed electrical work, start to finish.

Whether it's a single outlet or a full service upgrade, every job gets the same thing: licensed pros, clean work, and a circuit that's tested before we leave.

Service 01

Panel Upgrades & Service

Older panels can't keep up with modern loads — EV chargers, heat pumps, hot tubs. We upgrade your service safely and get it inspection-ready, with minimal downtime.

  • 100A to 200A service upgrades
  • Federal Pacific / fuse panel replacements
  • Sub-panels for garages, additions & shops
  • Surge protection & breaker swaps
  • ESA permit & inspection handled for you
Service 02

EV Charger Installation

We size your charger to your vehicle and your panel, run a clean dedicated circuit, and walk you through any available rebates so you're charging at full speed.

  • Level 2 (240V) home charger installs
  • Load calculations & panel assessment
  • Hardwired or NEMA 14-50 outlet options
  • Rebate & incentive guidance
  • Tidy conduit runs, indoor or outdoor
Service 03

Lighting & Fixtures

From pot lights that transform a room to smart switches you control from your phone — clean installs, no guesswork, and no unnecessary drywall damage.

  • Recessed / pot light installation
  • Fixture swaps, dimmers & smart switches
  • Under-cabinet & accent lighting
  • Outdoor, landscape & security lighting
  • Ceiling fans & bathroom exhaust fans
Service 04

Wiring & Renovations

Planning a reno? We rough-in new circuits, rewire old knob-and-tube, and coordinate with your contractor so the electrical never holds up your project.

  • New circuits & dedicated lines
  • Knob-and-tube & aluminum rewires
  • Kitchen, basement & addition rough-ins
  • Outlets, USB receptacles & GFCI/AFCI
  • Code corrections & pre-sale fixes

Commercial & Industrial
